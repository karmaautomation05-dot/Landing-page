Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Are sessions online or in-person?
+
All sessions are conducted online via Zoom or Google Meet, making them accessible to clients across India and globally. This means you get world-class therapy from the comfort and privacy of your own home.
How long before I see results?
+
Most clients notice meaningful shifts within the first 2-3 sessions. Deep, lasting transformation typically unfolds over 6-12 weeks. Healing is not linear, but you will feel the momentum early in our work together.
Is everything I share confidential?
+
Absolutely. Everything shared in our sessions is held in complete confidentiality under professional ethics guidelines. Your privacy and safety are my highest priority. I will never share your information without explicit consent.
Do you work with men too?
+
Yes! While my marketing often speaks to women, I work with all genders and identities. If you're resonating with my approach, I would love to connect with you regardless of your gender.
Can I reschedule or cancel sessions?
+
Yes, with at least 24 hours notice, sessions can be rescheduled at no extra cost. I understand life happens — flexibility is built into how I work so that healing remains sustainable and stress-free.
What if I'm not sure therapy is right for me?
+
That's exactly why I offer a free 30-minute discovery call — no pressure, no commitment. We'll have a real conversation about what you're experiencing and whether working together feels like the right fit.
